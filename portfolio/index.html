ABOUT ME

I'm a third year Computer Science & Engineering student from Government College of Engineering, Kannur. I've been in love with computers since the time I can remember. Being able to make it dance to my tune - or, you know, function according to my code - seemed like just the right step.

For the past couple years, I’ve been exploring my interests in robotics, web-development, and image processing.

CODE

I'm passionate about Computer Vision and Deep Learning. I've completed an internship at International Centre for Free and Open Source Software (an autonomous institute set up by the Govt. of Kerala), over the course of which I've worked on tasks involving image processing. I'm currently working on a visual extractor and parser for Adhaar cards.

I like carrying my learning forward - I’ve facilitated classes in C, Python, Arduino programming and Machine Learning using Tensorflow.

Some of my projects can be found on my Github.

Leadership

As Chairperson of the ISTE Chapter of GCEK, I initiated and led a team of girls to host our first 24 hour women-only hackathon, Hack the Night. I've written about my experience here.

While being a coordinator for the Women In Engineering affinity group in our college, I’ve coordinated techno-social events for women safety (Shein), as well as conducted sessions on engineering awareness for high-school students (Contessa).

I have also been the event host for TEDxGCEKannur on top of being a part of the curatorial team , the Head of Programming for the Robotics Club of GCEK, as well as the Joint Convenor of the Technical Committee for Xplore'19, the 5th National Level Techno-Management Cultural Festival of GCEK.

More of my work experiemce can be found on my Linkedin.
